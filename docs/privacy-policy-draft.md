# CardWise Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: May 22, 2026

CardWise is a US-only credit card rewards optimizer. This draft should be reviewed before publication.

## Information We Collect

CardWise may collect:

- Account information, such as email address, hashed password, and optional display name.
- App usage data needed to operate the wallet and recommendation experience.
- Credit cards selected by the user for their CardWise wallet.
- Subscription status and purchase-related identifiers from RevenueCat.
- Promo code redemption status.
- Device and diagnostic information provided by platform services, hosting logs, or crash reporting tools if enabled.

CardWise does not ask users to enter credit card account numbers, bank login credentials, Social Security numbers, or full payment card details.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Create and authenticate accounts.
- Save the cards users choose in their CardWise wallet.
- Recommend which saved card to use for selected purchase categories.
- Enforce Free and Premium plan limits.
- Sync subscription and promo code status.
- Diagnose bugs, API failures, and app reliability issues.

## Subscriptions And Payments

Subscriptions are handled through Apple App Store, Google Play, and RevenueCat. CardWise receives subscription status and related purchase metadata from RevenueCat, but does not directly process payment card details.

## Advertising

Free users may see ads. AdMob may process device and ad-related identifiers according to Google's policies. Internal test builds may show placeholder or test ad behavior.

## Data Storage

CardWise stores account and wallet data on hosted backend services. The mobile app may store local cache data on the device, including card catalog data and session tokens. Tokens are stored using secure storage where available.

## Data Sharing

We may share data with service providers that help operate the app, including backend hosting, database hosting, subscription management, app store services, and advertising providers. We do not sell personal information.

## Financial Disclaimer

CardWise provides rewards optimization estimates for informational purposes only. It is not financial, legal, tax, or credit advice. Card terms, fees, rewards, credits, and eligibility can change. Users should verify all card details with the issuer before applying for or using any credit card.

## User Choices

Users may:

- Log out from the app.
- Request account or data deletion by contacting support.
- Manage subscriptions through their App Store or Google Play account.
