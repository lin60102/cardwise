## Full Description

CardWise is a simple credit card rewards optimizer for US cardholders.

Add the cards you already own, choose a purchase category, and CardWise ranks your wallet so you can quickly see which card may earn the best cashback, points, miles, or annual value.

Free features include:

- Save up to 5 personal cards.
- Compare rewards by purchase category.
- See a best-card recommendation with explanation.
- Use a local card catalog fallback when available.

Premium features include:

- Unlimited saved cards.
- Business card support.
- No ads.
- Annual fee and credit optimization.
- Annual rewards dashboard.
- Personalized card planning tools.

CardWise supports common categories such as dining, groceries, gas, travel, flights, hotels, delivery, Amazon, Costco, Target, drugstores, and general purchases.

Financial disclaimer: CardWise provides estimates for informational purposes only. It is not financial, legal, tax, or credit advice. Credit card terms, rewards, annual fees, credits, caps, and eligibility may change. Always verify current terms with the issuer.
