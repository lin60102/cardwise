# CardWise Support

CardWise helps users choose the best saved credit card for common purchase categories.

## Contact

Email: `kensa.support@gmail.com`

Include:

- Your account email.
- Device model and iOS/Android version.
- App build number.
- A short description of the issue.
- Screenshot or screen recording if available.

## Common Issues

### The first login attempt is slow

The backend may need to wake up after inactivity. CardWise should show a retry message and try again automatically. If it still fails, wait a moment and try again.

### I cannot add more than 5 cards

Free accounts can save up to 5 cards. Premium unlocks unlimited saved cards.

### Business cards are locked

Business cards are a Premium feature.

### Purchases or restore purchases do not work

Confirm you are using the same App Store or Google Play account used to purchase. If the issue continues, contact support with your account email and build number.

### Recommendations look wrong

Reward values are estimates based on the CardWise card database. Issuer terms can change. Verify rates, caps, credits, and annual fees directly with the card issuer.
